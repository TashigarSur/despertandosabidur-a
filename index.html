Tashigar South

Meditative experiences by themselves are not enough in the face of challenging reality. Above all, the ability to be able to live our daily lives with a full presence is the essence for a harmonious existence.

We invite you to meet in Tashigar Sur, a beautiful land in the mountains of Cordoba, to celebrate life and awaken wisdom.
